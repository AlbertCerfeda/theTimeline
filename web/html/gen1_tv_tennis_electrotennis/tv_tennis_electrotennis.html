TV TENNIS ELECTROTENNIS

History

The Tv Tennis Electrotennis is a home dedicate console appertaining to the first generation. It was released by Epoch Co. together with Magnavox on September 12th 1975, several months before the Home Pong in America, only in Japan becoming the first video game console ever launched in Japan.
Its cost was 19 000 yen and sold about 10 000 units.

Console

This console implemented the Pong game but added a unique feature: which was the UHF antenna, that allowed it to connect wirelessly to the TV. There also exists a model not famous at all with a silver-grey cover, as shown in the figure.
